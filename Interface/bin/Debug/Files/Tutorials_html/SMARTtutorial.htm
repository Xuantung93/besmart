SMART TUTORIAL

 

SMART method:

This method is very simple. You have to attribute the importance of each criterion, by assign 10 points to the least important attribute and more then 10 points reflecting the importance of the criteria relative to the least important attribute. The highest value corresponds to the criterion of greater importance.

 

To illustrate better the algorithm, we are going to consider three criteria: cost of a license, compatibility between operating systems and user interaction. The table below represents an example of scale that can be implemented.
